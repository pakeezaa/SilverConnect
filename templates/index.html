About SilverConnect

SilverConnect is your welcoming space for accessible technology and digital confidence.

A patient, zero-pressure environment where seniors learn everyday digital tools with friendly guidance from local student volunteers. Break down barriers and stay beautifully connected to the people who matter most.

Our core support

Patient Guidance

Learn at your own pace with friendly, face-to-face support that never rushes you.

Everyday Tech

Master everyday tools — WhatsApp video calls, tablets, smartphones, smart TVs.

Safe Browsing

Recognize online scams, manage strong passwords, and browse the web safely.

Community

Get paired with a kind, tech-savvy student volunteer right in your neighbourhood.

Tech Help, Made Human.

Are you a student looking to make a meaningful difference? Spend an hour giving free, patient tech support to seniors in your community.
