About

Bridging the digital divide with patience, empathy, and local connection.

A space for every generation

SilverConnect Hub bridges seniors and tech-savvy students. Families easily find patient helpers nearby, while students effortlessly list their availability.

Every connection means a senior staying in touch with grandchildren, navigating online banking independently, and regaining confidence in a digital world.

What we stand for

Patience

Technology help should never feel rushed. Our volunteers are selected for empathy and willingness to go at a senior's pace.

Local

Every connection is neighbourhood-based. We believe in the power of local community and face-to-face human interaction.

Free

Tech support through SilverConnect Hub is 100% free, always. No hidden fees, no subscriptions — just community kindness.

Trust

We encourage families to be present during sessions and volunteers to bring a positive, respectful attitude to every interaction.

Confidence

Our goal isn't dependency — it's confidence. We teach seniors so they can do it themselves next time.

Mutual

Volunteers gain communication skills, service hours, and the profound satisfaction of making a real difference.
